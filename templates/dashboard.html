Instructions

How to Use

  1. Select an interviewee to enter the interrogation room
  2. Click "Start Speaking" to record your question
  3. Click "Stop Speaking" to submit — analysis runs automatically
  4. The GSR waveform displays the AI suspect's real-time response
  5. View question history at any time

Tips

  • Speak clearly and close to the microphone
  • Keep each question under 30 seconds
  • Clear, specific questions yield clearer GSR signals
  • Save your session when finished
